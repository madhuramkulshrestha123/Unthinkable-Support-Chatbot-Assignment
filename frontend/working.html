How It Works

Demo Video

Watch this demo to see how the Unthinkable Support Chatbot works in action. See how to select customers, start chats, and get personalized support based on order history.

Step 1: Select a Customer

Choose a customer from the customer list. Each customer profile contains their order history and preferences.

Step 2: Start Chatting

Click the "Chat" button on the customer card to initiate a support session. The AI assistant will have access to the customer's context.

Step 3: Receive Personalized Support

The AI chatbot provides personalized responses based on the customer's order history, preferences, and current query.

Step 4: Context-Aware Responses

All responses are tailored to the specific customer, referencing their past orders, payment methods, and delivery history.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Node.js with Express
  • AI Processing: Google's Gemini API
  • Data Storage: In-memory storage (extensible to MongoDB)
